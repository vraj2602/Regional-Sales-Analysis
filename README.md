## üìù Short Description / Purpose
The **USA Regional Sales Analysis Dashboard** is a comprehensive Power BI report designed to provide Acme Co.‚Äôs leadership with actionable insights into their sales ecosystem. ## üí° Business Impact & Insights
* **Regional Strategy:** Identified that while California is the market leader, states like Illinois and Texas represent significant secondary growth hubs with high order density.
* **Seasonal Planning:** Detected a recurring volume uptick in May‚ÄìJune and a notable dip in early 2017, allowing management to better align inventory and marketing spend with seasonal cycles.
* **Pricing Optimization:** Python analysis confirmed no strong correlation between unit price and margin, suggesting a successful tiered pricing strategy that protects profitability across different price points.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Pinpointed the "AXW291" warehouse as a critical logistical hub, handling a disproportionate amount of volume.
